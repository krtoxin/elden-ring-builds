Master Every Build in the Lands Between

Builds in Elden Ring are powerful combinations of stats, weapons, armor, and spells tailored to unique playstyles. Whether you’re melting bosses with magic or tanking blows for allies, each build offers a distinct path to mastering the game.

From agile glass-cannon mages to holy tanks and dual-wielding berserkers, this hub brings together curated builds optimized for Shadow of the Erdtree, New Game+, and both PvE and PvP encounters.

You’ll find gear recommendations, stat priorities, spell selections, and strategies that make each build shine — no matter your experience level.

How to Respec in Elden Ring?

After defeating Rennala at Raya Lucaria, you’ll unlock the Rebirth mechanic. This allows you to respec your character by spending a Larval Tear, enabling flexibility to try out different builds without starting over.

Respec Info: Beat Rennala, use Larval Tear to reset stats.
PvE & PvP Ready: These builds are tested in both solo and multiplayer modes.
Note: Aim for 50–60 Vigor for best survivability.

Paladin Build Beginner

A tanky healer who can buff and defend allies, allowing them to take the brunt of blows while their team burns enemies down.

  • ClassConfessor
  • Flask SpreadMostly HP with 1–2 FP
  • WeaponOne-handed + Sacred Seal
  • ShieldBrass Shield
  • ArmorMid-roll heavy gear
  • Primary StatsFaith & Vigor
  • Secondary StatsEndurance & Strength
  • SkillsBarricade Shield, Golden Vow
  • SpellsUrgent Heal, Heal

Before combat, summon your Spirit Ashes and buff both yourself and them with Golden Vow for bonus damage and defense.

Use Barricade Shield to absorb attacks while allies or summons attack freely. Focus on Faith for healing and stat scaling.

Once you acquire Heal, support your team even more by increasing survivability and team uptime in boss fights.

Templar Build Level 50

A tanky melee-focused Paladin that blocks, buffs, and smashes. Heals allies, stuns enemies with counters, and brings holy pressure to the field.

  • ClassConfessor
  • Flask SpreadMostly HP, some FP
  • WeaponNoble's Slender Sword
  • ShieldBrass Shield
  • ArmorMausoleum Knight Armor, Greathelm, Knight Greaves & Gauntlets
  • Primary StatsFaith & Endurance
  • Secondary StatsVigor
  • SkillsGolden Vow, Sacred Blade, Barricade Shield
  • SpellsBlessing's Boon, Crucible: Tail

Use Block Counters after shielding enemy attacks to stagger and punish foes. It often opens up critical hits or does solid damage on its own.

Activate Barricade Shield to reduce stamina drain while blocking. This gives you more chances to counter safely and often.

In co-op, buff your team and summons with Golden Vow. For solo play, use Sacred Blade for a ranged holy slash and bonus damage.

Blessing's Boon grants passive healing over time — perfect for keeping you and your team topped off mid-fight.

Use Aspects of the Crucible: Tail to knock down large enemies and clear mobs. Hold the cast button for a double sweep and increased stagger.
